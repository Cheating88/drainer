FAQ
What is a "dust" or residual balance?
Tiny token amounts left over from swaps, airdrops, or failed transactions. Collectively they can reach hundreds of dollars.
Is my wallet safe?
Read-only mode only. We never request private keys, seed phrases, or signing permissions.
Which networks are supported?
Ethereum, Solana, BNB Smart Chain, and Aptos. Arbitrum, Polygon and Base coming soon.
